FAQ

Questions we get asked

Is LocalPulse free?
+
Yes — completely free during early access. Once we launch fully, there will be a free tier with limited reports and a paid plan with unlimited insights (~₹299/month).
Which cities are currently covered?
+
We are live with Mumbai and Nashik. Pune, Nagpur, Aurangabad, Surat, and Ahmedabad are coming next. Join the waitlist and tell us your city — it helps us prioritise.
How accurate is the data?
+
We pull from Google Trends (location-filtered), Google Maps (competitor count), Zomato/Swiggy (demand signals), and social media trends. During early access we also verify manually and update every week.
I live in a small town. Does it work for me?
+
Absolutely — smaller cities have the biggest opportunities. The whole idea is to spot what's trending in Tier 1 cities before it reaches your town. That timing gap is where you win.
Do I need business experience?
+
Not at all. LocalPulse is built for first-time founders and aspiring entrepreneurs. Each opportunity includes startup cost estimates and a basic first-steps roadmap. Zero experience needed.
When does the full app launch?
+
We're targeting mid-2026 for the full launch. Everyone on the waitlist gets early access before public launch, plus a founding member discount on any paid plan.
